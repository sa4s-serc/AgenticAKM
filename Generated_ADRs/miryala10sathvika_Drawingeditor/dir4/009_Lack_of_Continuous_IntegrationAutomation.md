# ADR-009: Lack of Continuous Integration/Automation

**Date**: 2025-10-13
**Status**: Proposed

## Context
No CI configuration is visible in the provided structure and no tests are present to drive CI.

## Decision
Rely on manual local execution and verification rather than automated pipelines.

## Consequences
Inconsistent quality checks, slower feedback cycles, harder enforcement of standards, and increased risk of broken mainline code.
