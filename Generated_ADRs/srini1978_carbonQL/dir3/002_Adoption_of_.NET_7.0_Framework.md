# ADR-002: Adoption of .NET 7.0 Framework

**Date**: 2025-10-09
**Status**: Proposed

## Context
The project needed a modern, performant, and cross-platform technology stack to ensure long-term viability and flexibility in deployment environments.

## Decision
C# on the .NET 7.0 framework was selected as the primary technology for all components of the solution.

## Consequences
The project benefits from modern language features, high performance, and cross-platform compatibility (Windows, macOS, Linux). This choice positions the application on a contemporary and well-supported platform, simplifying future development and hiring.
