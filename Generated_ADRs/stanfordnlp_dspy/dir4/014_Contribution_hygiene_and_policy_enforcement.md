# ADR-014: Contribution hygiene and policy enforcement

**Date**: 2025-10-13
**Status**: Proposed

## Context
Pre-commit config, LICENSE, CONTRIBUTING, issue/PR templates included.

## Decision
Enforce style and process gates before merge and provide clear contribution guidelines.

## Consequences
Pros: higher baseline quality, consistent repo standards, smoother triage. Cons: added contributor friction, need to maintain lint/format rules across tools.
